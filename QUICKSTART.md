# 🎮 CoinDrop - Quick Start Guide

## ✅ How to Play RIGHT NOW (No Setup Needed!)

The game is **100% ready to play** in demo mode!

### Steps:

1. **Open the game**: http://localhost:8080

2. **You'll see a purple banner** saying "Playing in Demo Mode"

3. **Use the controls**:
   - 🎯 **Drop Angle**: Slide to choose angle (0-360°)
   - 💪 **Drop Force**: Slide to choose force (Soft/Medium/Hard)
   - 🎮 **Drop Coin**: Click to drop!

4. **Watch the magic**:
   - Realistic physics simulation
   - Water particle effects
   - Coin bouncing and deflection
   - Win/loss animations

5. **Try to win**: Land the coin in the small glass!

## ⚠️ Important: DON'T Click "Connect Wallet"

The "Connect Wallet" button is hidden because:
- Monad network is not publicly available yet
- Clicking it will show an RPC error
- **You don't need it!** The game works perfectly without it
