## 🚀 Enable GitHub Pages (2 Minutes)

Follow these simple steps to make your website live:

### Step 1: Go to Repository Settings
1. Open your browser and go to: **https://github.com/manikant1446/DropCoinGame**
2. Click on **Settings** tab (top right of the page)

### Step 2: Enable GitHub Pages
1. In the left sidebar, click on **Pages** (under "Code and automation")
2. Under **Source**, select **Deploy from a branch**
3. Under **Branch**, select:
   - Branch: **main**
   - Folder: **/ (root)**
4. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Wait for Deployment (1-2 minutes)
- GitHub will build and deploy your site
- Refresh the Pages settings page after a minute
- You'll see a message: "Your site is live at https://manikant1446.github.io/DropCoinGame/"
